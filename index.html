Welcome to the Card Battle Game!

This is a two-player game where you and a friend take turns to reduce each other's health to zero. Let’s dive in!

How to Play

1. Click "Create Room" to generate a code and share it with your friend.

2. Your friend enters the code and clicks "Join Room" to start.

3. Take turns dragging cards to the play slot or burn slot, then click "End Turn."

What Are Cards?

Cards are your tools to fight your opponent. Each card has:

  • Type: Rock, Paper, or Scissors (Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock).
  • Damage: Top right number—how much damage it deals if it wins.
  • Mana Cost: Bottom left number—mana needed to play it.
  • Abilities: Optional effects like Heal, Debuff, or Shield.

Your Turn Options

  • Play a Card: Drag to the play slot (costs mana).
  • Burn a Card: Drag to the burn slot (gains mana).

Mana Rules

  • Start with 3 mana, gain 3 per turn (resets to 3 if you end at 0).
  • Max mana is 15.

Tips

Your opponent’s hand is visible—use it to plan your moves!

Ready? Outsmart your friend and win!
