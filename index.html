Welcome to the Card Battle Game!

This tutorial will teach you the basics, step by step. Your goal is to reduce your opponent’s health to zero before they do the same to you. Let’s dive in!

What Are Cards?

Cards are your tools to fight your opponent. Each card in your hand has:

  • Type: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. It works like the classic game:
    • Rock beats Scissors.
    • Scissors beat Paper.
    • Paper beats Rock.
  • Damage: The number at the top right of the card. This shows how much damage it deals if it wins the matchup.
  • Mana Cost: The number at the bottom left. This is how much mana you need to play it.
  • Abilities: Some cards have extra effects like Heal, Debuff, or Shield.

How to Use Your Cards

You have two options each turn:

  • Playing a Card: Drag a card to the play slot to use it. This costs mana equal to the card’s mana cost.
  • Burning a Card: Drag a card to the burn slot to discard it and gain mana equal to its mana cost.

Important: Once you drag a card to the play slot, it’s locked in—you can’t undo it.

Managing Mana

Mana is your energy for playing cards and making moves:

  • You start with 3 mana and gain 3 more each turn (unless you ended with 0 mana, then it resets to 3).
  • Spend mana to play cards or burn cards to gain more mana.

Tips for Beginners

The opponent’s hand is always visible. Use your mana wisely to counter their cards with the best possible combination of your own.

That’s it! You’re ready to play. Good luck outsmarting your opponent!
